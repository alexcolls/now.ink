## Getting Started

### 1. Install the App

**Mobile (iOS/Android):**
- Download from App Store / Google Play (coming soon)
- Grant camera and location permissions

**Web:**
- Visit [now.ink](https://now.ink)
- Works on desktop and mobile browsers

### 2. Connect Your Wallet

You need a Solana wallet to mint NFTs and prove ownership.

**Recommended Wallets:**
- **Phantom** (most popular, easy setup)
- **Solflare** (advanced features)

**Steps:**
1. Install Phantom wallet extension or app
2. Create a new wallet or import existing
3. **SAVE YOUR SEED PHRASE** (12-24 words) safely
4. In now.ink, click "Connect Wallet"
5. Approve connection in Phantom

**First Time?**
- Get ~0.05 SOL from an exchange (Coinbase, Binance)
- You'll need it for NFT minting fees (~$0.20/mint)

## Recording & Live Streaming

### Start Recording

1. **Open now.ink mobile app**
2. Tap **camera icon** on home screen
3. **Allow location access** (required for geo-tagging)
4. Tap **red record button** to start streaming

### While Streaming

- **Live viewers:** See count at top (if public)
- **Duration:** Timer shows recording length
- **Stop:** Tap red button again to end

### After Recording

You'll see two options:

**Option 1: Discard**
- Video deleted, no NFT created
- No cost

**Option 2: Save as NFT**
- Video uploaded to Arweave (permanent storage)
- NFT minted on Solana blockchain
- Cost: ~$0.20 + platform commission (TBD)
- Takes ~30 seconds to complete

## Minting NFTs

### What Gets Minted?

When you save a video, an NFT is created with:
- **Video:** Stored forever on Arweave
- **Thumbnail:** Auto-generated preview image
- **Metadata:**
  - GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
  - Timestamp (exact date/time)
  - Your wallet address (creator)
  - Video duration

### Minting Process

```
1. You tap "Save as NFT"
   ↓
2. Video uploads to Arweave
   (shows progress %)
   ↓
3. NFT mints on Solana
   (you'll see blockchain transaction)
   ↓
4. Done! NFT appears in your wallet
   (and on the map for others to discover)
```

### Costs

| Item | Cost (approx) |
|------|--------------|
| Arweave storage | $0.17 (40 MB video) |
| Solana mint fee | $0.001 |
| Platform commission | TBD |
| **Total** | **~$0.20+** |

**Free users:** 100 mints/day  
**Premium users:** Unlimited mints

## Discovering Moments

### The Map Interface

**Web app:** Main feature is an interactive map showing all NFT pins.

**How to Use:**
1. Pan and zoom the map (like Google Maps)
2. See **glowing pins** = geo-tagged NFTs
3. Click a pin to see preview (thumbnail + info)
4. Click "Play" to watch the video

### Time Slider

Filter moments by time range:

- **Today:** See what's happening right now
- **Last Week:** Recent moments
- **Last Month:** Scroll back in time
- **Custom:** Drag slider to any date range

Example: "Show me all moments from Times Square on New Year's Eve 2024"

### Playback Rules

**Free Users:**
- ✅ Watch NFTs if you're **within 100m** of the location
- ❌ Can't watch distant NFTs

**Premium Users:**
- ✅ Watch **any NFT globally**
- No distance restrictions

**Why?** Encourages exploration of real places, not just scrolling from couch.

## Privacy Settings

### Public vs Private Moments

When saving an NFT, choose:

**Public (default):**
- ✅ Appears on map for all users
- ✅ Shows in your profile
- ✅ Followers see in their feed
- ✅ NFT is tradable/sellable

**Private:**
- ❌ Not on public map
- ✅ Only you can see it
- ✅ Still minted on blockchain (you own it)
- ❌ Can't be sold (unless you make it public later)

**Friends-Only (coming soon):**
- Only followers can see it

### Changing Privacy Later

- Go to NFT detail page
- Click **"Change Privacy"**
- Select Public/Private
- Confirm transaction (small SOL fee)

### What's Always Public

- Your wallet address (on blockchain)
- Your username and profile pic
- Number of followers/following
- NFTs you've made public

### What's Never Shared

- Your location history (only individual NFT locations)
- Your email or personal data (we don't collect it)
- Your viewing history

## Wallet Management

### Your Wallet = Your Identity

- **No email, no password.** Just your wallet.
- Your wallet address is your username (e.g., `7xKXtg...`)
- You can set a custom username (optional)

### Security Tips

1. **Never share your seed phrase** (12-24 words)
2. **Use a hardware wallet** for large amounts of SOL/NFTs
3. **Enable biometrics** in Phantom app
4. **Double-check** wallet address when sending funds

### Losing Access

If you lose your seed phrase:
- ❌ You lose access to your wallet forever
- ❌ You lose all your NFTs
- ❌ now.ink cannot recover your wallet

**This is Web3.** You own your data, but you're responsible for it.

### Disconnecting Wallet

- Click **"Disconnect"** in settings
- App logs you out
- Reconnect anytime with same wallet

## Why now.ink?

### Anti-AI

**We reject AI-generated content.**

- If it wasn't captured live on your camera, it doesn't exist here
- No filters, no face-swap, no generated videos
- Authenticity is the only currency

### Anti-Algorithm

**You follow who you want. You see what they post. End of story.**

- No "For You" page with mystery recommendations
- No shadowbanning or suppressing posts
- No pay-to-reach engagement games
- Chronological feed, always

### Anti-Corporate

**No ads. No data mining. No selling your attention.**

- Revenue from small platform commission on mints
- Optional premium tier for power users
- Your data stays yours (it's on your wallet, not our servers)

### Pro-Truth

**Open-source by design.**

- All code is auditable on GitHub
- You can fork it, run your own instance
- No black-box algorithms deciding what's "true"

### Pro-You

**You own your content, forever.**

- NFTs are on Solana blockchain (not just our database)
- Videos stored on Arweave (permanent, decentralized)
- Even if now.ink shuts down, your NFTs live on

## FAQs

**Q: Do I need crypto knowledge to use now.ink?**  
A: Not really. Just install Phantom, get a bit of SOL, and you're good.

**Q: Can I delete my NFTs?**  
A: No. Once on Arweave, it's permanent. Choose wisely.

**Q: What if I record something embarrassing?**  
A: Tap "Discard" instead of "Save as NFT". Never minted = never on-chain.

**Q: Can I edit videos before minting?**  
A: No. That's the point—raw, unfiltered reality.

**Q: Is my location tracked constantly?**  
A: No. Only captured at the moment you start recording. Not tracked otherwise.

**Q: Can I make money on now.ink?**  
A: Yes. Mint rare moments (e.g., breaking news, historic events) and sell them on NFT marketplaces.

**Q: What if someone mints something illegal?**  
A: We'll implement community reporting + manual review. Details TBD.
